Here’s The Reality

Every business has spare capacity and resources that are not being monetized, wasting unimaginable amounts of money every year.

By joining the closed-loop BarterPay Community, you can obtain value for expiring time and idle inventory, from brand new customers, and then get back what you need all while keeping your cash in the bank!

We’re proud to have the support of our partners. Our alignment with these organizations helps us expand our network which creates tremendous value for our members across Canada.

Need a Loan?

Want to obtain a good or service from the network but don’t have enough Barter Credits™?

We can loan you Barter Credits™ in advance so you can get what you need now and then pay it off later by bartering your extra products and services into the system.

It’s Canada’s newest way to obtain business financing!
